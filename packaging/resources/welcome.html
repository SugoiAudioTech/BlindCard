Blind Card

Version 1.0.0

Welcome to the Blind Card installer.

Blind Card is an ear training plugin that helps you improve your audio recognition skills through gamified exercises.

This installer will guide you through the installation of:

  • AU Plugin - For Logic Pro, GarageBand
  • VST3 Plugin - For Ableton Live, Cubase, etc.
  • Standalone App - No DAW required
