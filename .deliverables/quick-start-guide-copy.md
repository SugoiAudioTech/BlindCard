## 標題
Quick Start Guide

## 開場說明（一句話定位）
Blind Card lets you compare audio tracks without knowing which is which — eliminating bias from your decisions.

## 步驟

### 1
Insert Blind Card on each track you want to compare (2-8 tracks)

### 2
Choose a rating mode:
- ⭐ **Stars** – Rate each track from 1-5 stars
- 🎯 **Guess** – Identify which plugin/mix is on each card
- ❓ **Q&A** – Find a specific track when prompted

### 3
Click "SHUFFLE" to randomize and hide track identities

### 4
Click cards to listen and rate (or use ← → arrow keys)

### 5
Click "REVEAL" to see results and actual track names

### 6
Click "RESET" to start a new test

## Tip 區塊
💡 **Tip**
Enable "Auto Gain" to level-match all tracks, ensuring fair comparison without volume bias.
